WiFi Tester — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

WiFi Tester ("the app") is developed by Hall Software.

Data Collection

WiFi Tester does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information displayed by the app — including nearby WiFi network names, signal strengths, channel information, and hardware addresses — is read directly from your device's WiFi radio and is never sent off your device.

Permissions

The app requests location permission on Android. This permission is required by Google's Android operating system to access WiFi scan results. The app does not use this permission to determine your location and does not share location data with anyone.

Third Parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.
